### FAQs

- How does CloudBolt unify reporting across hybrid cloud environments?

CloudBolt normalizes cost and usage data from AWS, Azure, GCP, VMware, Nutanix, and more into a FOCUS-compliant format, enabling true side-by-side comparisons and eliminating data fragmentation.

- Can reporting be customized for different stakeholders?

Yes. CloudBolt delivers role-specific dashboards so executives, engineers, and FinOps teams each see the insights they need—from high-level KPIs to workload-level detail.

- How accurate is the cost data?

Costs are shown using your actual negotiated rates, credits, and discounts—not list prices—so you always have a true representation of your spend. Data updates hourly for near real-time accuracy.

- Does it support SaaS cost visibility in addition to IaaS?

Absolutely. Through our CloudEagle integration, CloudBolt provides visibility into 500+ SaaS applications, including shadow IT discovery, license reclamation, and unified reporting with your cloud infrastructure spend.
