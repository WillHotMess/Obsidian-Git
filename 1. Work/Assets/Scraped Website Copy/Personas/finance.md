Pre-deployment financial controls

Budget-aware automation

Define spending limits that trigger automated actions—from alerts to usage restrictions. Roll up budgets across org units with visibility into every level.

Invoice automation

Automatically generate chargeback invoices based on your allocation rules, complete with markups, discounts, and commitment share calculations.

Predict 90 days out with AI-powered forecasts. Model what-if scenarios, track variance, and get notified when spend is trending off course.
